The Port Call Timeline

A streamlined, four-stage process for guaranteed port efficiency and swift cargo handling.

Phase 1

Pre-Arrival Planning

Vessel clearance documents are submitted, and berth reservations are finalized. We liaise with marine services (tugs, pilots) to confirm the estimated time of arrival (ETA) and optimize the entry schedule.

Phase 2

On-Dock Operations

Once berthed, customs, immigration, and port health clearances are expedited. Cargo manifests are synchronized with terminal systems to prepare for immediate discharge/loading.

Phase 3

Efficient Cargo Movement

Supervision of stevedoring, ensuring safe and correct cargo handling. We coordinate the immediate transfer of cleared containers to designated storage or transport trucks.

Phase 4

Post-Departure Audit

Finalization of Disbursement Accounts (DA) and port dues. A comprehensive post-call report is submitted, detailing all activities, times, and costs to the vessel owner.
